INVICTUS CLUB


Welcome to the Invictus Club at MIT Academy of Engineering, where innovation meets technology in the exciting world of robotics! Our club is a vibrant community of students passionate about exploring, building, and advancing robotics technology. Here's a glimpse into what makes Invictus Club unique


Robotics Workshops: We regularly organize workshops to introduce members to various aspects of robotics, from basic concepts to advanced programming and hardware integration. Project Development: Members have the opportunity to work on hands-on projects, applying their knowledge to design and build robots.

These projects cover a wide range of applications, from automation and artificial intelligence to drone technology. Competitions: Invictus Club encourages participation in robotics competitions, both within the university and external events. This allows members to showcase their skills, learn from others, and gain valuable experience in a competitive setting.
